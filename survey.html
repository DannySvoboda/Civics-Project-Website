Civics Project: Technology and Computer Science Education

Survey

"

The Data of Question 1 shows that there is a significant drop in the percentage of students who choose technology and computer science classes in high school, compared to the first 8 grades.

"

The Data of Question 2 shows a almost even split in whether students enjoy technology or computer science classes that they have taken.

"

The Data of Question 3 shows a variety of topics from different classes. The ranking topics were programming, video game creation, and robotics.

"

The Data of Question 4 shows a clear majority vote that technology needs an expanded focus with regards to new advancements in modern technology and its prevalence.

"

About half of the students did not answer this question, and of those who did, the most popular answer was a variation of "no". Among the remaining responses, popular responses were code.org and hour of code, as well as scratch, and other resources seen in the district's curriculum.
